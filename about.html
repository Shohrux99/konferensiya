About the Conference

The 15th UMT Annual Symposium 2021 (UMTAS 2021) aims to virtually bring together scholars, professionals and postgraduate students to exchange and share their experiences and research results encompassing every facet of science and technology. With the theme “Acculturating sustainable research in pandemic paradigms”, the symposium would enable the participants to engage in thought-provoking discussions on their research amongst fellow students, speakers and renowned academic experts. In addition, the symposium would expose the participants to the current ideas, theories and methodologies in their research areas. It is also hoped that the symposium would carve an opportunity for researchers, academic experts, and students, to foster close relationships and strong research partnerships ahead.

In the spirit of embracing changes and assimilating new ideas during this pandemic era, the Faculty of Ocean Engineering Technology and Informatics (FTKKI), Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT), is organizing a proactive learning experience through UMTAS 2021 in which to be held on 23rd-25th November 2021. This symposium will enable scholars, professionals and postgraduate students from the science and technology field, to have the opportunity to present, share and discuss their discoveries from various perspectives. All theoretical, empirical and practical papers shall be shared throughout the symposium. The forthcoming 15th UMTAS 2021 is organised by FTKKI, UMT Malaysia and this year symposium is the latest series of UMTAS symposiums held by UMT in which the tradition started since 2002. The previous series of UMTAS can be viewed here.

Conference Objectives

  • Provides a field of sharing and exchange of expertise, presentation of the latest research findings and proliferation of ideas for the stated fields between academia, researchers and local and international professionals
  • Build a network of relationships and strengthen professional relationships between all participants and related industries
  • Introduce UMT and Malaysia to the international level as a regional hub of studies related to the field and theme of the university.
  • Introduce UMT and Malaysia to the international level as a regional hub of studies related to the field and theme of the university.
